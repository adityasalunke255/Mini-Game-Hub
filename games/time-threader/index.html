    How to Play — Time-Threader

    Draw a glowing path to guide moving events safely along the timeline.

    • Tap/Click and drag on the canvas to draw your path.
    • Events touching your path become safe and score when they exit.
    • Avoid collisions between events — collisions reduce your score.
    • Run lasts 20 seconds. Score = routed × 100 − collisions × 50.
